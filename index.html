A Little About SAG

Over the last few decades Artificial Insemination (AI) has been instrumental in increasing milk yield, and thereby farmersâ€™ income in the country. One of the signature benefits of AI is that this method that has the potential of rapid dissemination of elite male genetic material over a large geographical area. Even though AI is very powerful and useful technique for increasing the productivity of indigenous animals, the backbone for an effective AI program is the quality of the semen available.

As more and more farmers are adopting AI the demand for quality semen has grown exponentially in the country. This has led to the advent of very high-priced semen as well as duplicate semen from various spurious sources.

In light of this NDDB Dairy Services, a fully owned subsidiary of NDDB, with its deep-rooted commitment to provide affordable high-quality genetics to the dairy farmers, through its Semen Stations.Sabarmathi Ashram Gaushala (Bidaj, Ahmedabad Gujarat); Animal Breeding Center (Salon, Raebareli, Uttar Pradesh); Alamadhi Semen Station (Red Hills, Chennai, Tamil Nadu) and Rahuri Semen Station (Rahuri, Ahmednagar, Maharashtra) are the top semen stations in the country. The semen produced from these semen stations are marketed under the brand Superior Animal Genetics (SAG).

SAG STRAW

The SAG straw is unique in itself and is designed in such a way to differentiate it from imitators. SAG Semen Stations is using Florescent TBS straws, imported from France, which is not available with any other semen stations in India. Each dose of the SAG semen would carry a unique alpha-numeric code which when messaged to a stipulated mobile number (+91-99003 99000) will generate a reply message stating whether the semen dose used is genuine or counterfeit. Additionally, we are the only semen brand in the world that packs its semen in fluorescent straws. When a light of a particular wavelength falls on these straws it emits a fluorescent light. These additional features ensure that the farmers receive the authentic quality product.

Unique Alpha-Numeric Code

Each SAG frozen semen straw has a unique alpha numeric code printed on it. To check the authenticity of the straw, the unique code can be sent via an SMS to 9900399000. The user will receive alerts regarding the authenticity of the semen straw.
